I'm Naomi, a passionate individual on an exciting journey of career transformation. After years of swirling, sniffing, and selling the finest wines and spirits to bars and restaurants, I've decided to uncork a new chapter in my career and dive headfirst into the captivating realm of UX/UI design.

With a palette for aesthetics and a nose for problem-solving, I've discovered my knack for creating seamless user experiences and visually stunning interfaces. I believe that design has the power to shape the way we interact with the world, and I'm determined to make a positive impact through my work.

My transition from sales to design has been an adventure, filled with learning, growth, and a whole lot of creativity. I've honed my skills through intensive training and hands-on projects, immersing myself in the principles of user-centered design and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends. But it's not just about pixels and wireframes for me. I thrive on collaboration and believe in the power of teamwork to bring ideas to life. I'm a great listener, always eager to understand the needs and desires of users, and translate them into intuitive and visually captivating designs.

When I'm not immersed in the world of design, you can find me curled up with my dog and a good book, seeking inspiration in nature, or attempting to paint with watercolors. These passions fuel my creativity and help me approach design challenges from unique perspectives.

I'm not just a designer; I'm a storyteller, a problem-solver, and a master of pairing experiences. I believe that design, like a perfectly paired wine and dish, should elevate and enhance the user's journey. My sales expertise combined with my design skills give me a unique perspective to create impactful and user-centric solutions.

If you're looking for a dedicated and versatile UX/UI designer who can bring your vision to life, let's connect and make magic happen!
