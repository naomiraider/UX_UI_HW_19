I'm Naomi, a passionate individual on an exciting journey of career transformation. After years of swirling, sniffing, and selling the finest wines and spirits to bars and restaurants, I've decided to uncork a new chapter in my career and dive headfirst into the captivating realm of UX/UI design.

With a palette for aesthetics and a nose for problem-solving, I've discovered my knack for creating seamless user experiences and visually stunning interfaces. I believe that design has the power to shape the way we interact with the world, and I'm determined to make a positive impact through my work.

My transition from sales to design has been an adventure, filled with learning, growth, and a whole lot of creativity. I've honed my skills through intensive training and hands-on projects, immersing myself in the principles of user-centered design and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends. But it's not just about pixels and wireframes for me. I thrive on collaboration and believe in the power of teamwork to bring ideas to life. I'm a great listener, always eager to understand the needs and desires of users, and translate them into intuitive and visually captivating designs.

When I'm not immersed in the world of design, you can find me curled up with my dog and a good book, seeking inspiration in nature, or attempting to paint with watercolors. These passions fuel my creativity and help me approach design challenges from unique perspectives.

I'm not just a designer; I'm a storyteller, a problem-solver, and a master of pairing experiences. I believe that design, like a perfectly paired wine and dish, should elevate and enhance the user's journey. My sales expertise combined with my design skills give me a unique perspective to create impactful and user-centric solutions.

If you're looking for a dedicated and versatile UX/UI designer who can bring your vision to life, let's connect and make magic happen!

Testimonials

"Marilyn and Jim had the pleasure of working with Naomi, Mana, Joseph and Wilyza in the redesign of our website Hi5DogRescue.Org, a site specializing in the rescue of dogs from China. The initial website was created by a different Hi5DogRescue volunteer using the basic website tools of Godaddy. Naomi and team discussed the website with Marilyn and Jim to learn the organizations needs.

Naomi and team designed a website that exudes a sense of warmth and compassion, capturing the essence of the Hi5DogRescues mission. The overall design is clean, intuitive, and visually appealing, making it easy for users to navigate and find relevant information. The color scheme and imagery perfectly convey the organization's dedication to saving dogs and finding them forever homes.

One standout feature of the website is its user-friendly layout. The information is well-organized into distinct sections, allowing visitors to quickly access different areas of interest. The homepage effectively highlights links to adoptable dogs, success stories, and how to volunteer or donate creating a well-rounded experience for users. The integration of social media buttons allows users to easily share content, spreading the nonprofit's message further.

The team has effectively incorporated detailed profiles of each dog available for adoption, providing potential adopters with all the necessary information to make an informed decision. In addition, resources such as adoption guidelines, volunteer opportunities, and donation options are prominently displayed, encouraging interested users a path to participation/volunteering.

Lastly, the designers' collaboration with Marilyn, the website owner, is evident in every aspect. The site is a true reflection of the nonprofit's values and goals, showcasing Marilyn's satisfaction with the work performed. It is clear that Naomi and her team genuinely understood the vision and mission of hi5dogrescue.org, resulting in a successful partnership.

In conclusion, the performance of Naomi and her team in designing hi5dogrescue.org is praiseworthy. The website effectively conveys the nonprofit's mission, boasts a user-friendly layout, and offers valuable content for visitors. I have no doubt that this website will continue to aid the organization in its efforts to save and rehome dogs and provides a strong base from which the Hi5Dogrescue team can build."

Marilyn Founder, Hi5 Dog Rescue
